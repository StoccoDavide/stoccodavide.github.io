ABOUT ME

Over the past years, I have focused on solving real-world problems by combining theoretical methods with practical implementation. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in mechatronics and systems engineering, with research interests in numerical methods, symbolic computing, optimization, computational geometry, and automatic code generation for the efficient simulation of dynamical systems governed by ODEs and DAEs in the context of IVPs and BVPs. Motivated by challenging problems and eager to explore new methodologies, I am now seeking a position in a dynamic and research-driven environment that values innovation, where my attention to detail and analytical mindset can contribute positively.
