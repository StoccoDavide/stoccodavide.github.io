ABOUT ME

During my recent work, I have risen to the challenge of combining theoretical approaches with practical implementation. At the moment I am working as a postdoctoral researcher in mechatronics and systems engineering with a special interest in numerical methods, computational geometry, symbolic computing, and automatic code generation for efficient simulation of dynamical systems described by ODEs or DAEs in the context of IVPs and BVPs. Eager to explore new fields and methodologies, especially when they aim at solving challenging problems, I am currently looking for a position in a dynamic, open and research-driven environment that focuses on innovative projects, where my precise attitude and engineering mindset are seen as an added value.
